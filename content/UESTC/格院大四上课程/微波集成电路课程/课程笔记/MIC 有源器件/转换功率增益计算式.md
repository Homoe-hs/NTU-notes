$$
G_{T}=\dfrac{P_{L}}{P_{avs}} = \frac{(1-\left|\Gamma_S\right|^2)}{\left|1-\Gamma_S\Gamma_{\mathrm{in}}\right|^2}\left|S_{21}\right|^2 \dfrac{1-\left|\Gamma_L\right|^2}{\left|1-S_{22}\Gamma_L\right|^2}
$$
$P_{L}$ 是负载耗散的功率；$P_{avs}$ 是源的资用功率。
