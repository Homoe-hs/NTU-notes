## 特点
- ARM®32-bit Cortex®-M4 CPU
- 80 MHz max CPU frequency
- 256 KB Flash
- 64 KB SRAM
- GPIO (20) with external interrupt capability
- 12-bit ADC with 12 channels
- Timers (4)
- I2C (2)
- USART (2)
- SPI (2)
