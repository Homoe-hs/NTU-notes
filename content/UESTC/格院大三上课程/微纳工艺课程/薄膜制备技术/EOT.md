# EOT

$t_ {ox \, eff}$ = $\dfrac {\varepsilon _ {ox}}{\varepsilon _ {high-k}}$ $t_ {high-k}$

$t_{ox\, eff}$ 为等效氧化层厚度；$\varepsilon _ {ox}$ 为氧化层（$SiO_{2}$）介电常数；$\varepsilon _ {high-k}$ 为高介电常数（high-k）材料；$t_{high-k}$ 为高介电常数材料的厚度。
